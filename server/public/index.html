SWAPI data

To get to the SWAPI data, make RESTful requests at /api

  • Films
  • Characters
  • Planets
  • Species
  • Starships
  • Transports
  • Vehicles
There are several many-to-many relations between these collections
URL Junction table Explanation
/api/films/:film_id/characters films_characters The characters in films. A film has many characters. A character can appear in many films.
/api/characters/:character_id/films films_characters The films for a character. Same as above but approaching from the characters side.
/api/films/:film_id/planets films_planets The planets in a film. A film has many planets. A planet can appear in many films.
/api/planets/:planet_id/films films_planets The films that feature a planet. Same as above but approaching from the planet side.
/api/films/:film_id/species films_species The species in films. A film has many species. A species can appear in many films.
/api/films/:film_id/starships films_starships The starships in films. A film has many starships. A starship can appear in many films.
/api/films/:film_id/vehicles films_vehicles The vehicles in films. A film has many vehicles. A vehicle can appear in many films.
/api/species/:species_id/characters species_characters The characters who are members of species. This one is weird (but it's how the data was presented). A character is a member of one species. A species has many characters.
/api/starships/:starship_id/characters starships_characters The starships and their pilots. A starship can have many pilots. A character can fly many starships.
/api/vehicles/:vehicle_id/characters vehicles_characters The vehicles and their drivers. A vehicle can have many drivers. A character can drive many vehicles.
/api/planets/:planet_id/characters None needed The characters who are from a planet. A planet can have many characters but each character can have only one homeworld.
